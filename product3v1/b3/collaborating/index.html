Collaborating with Other Users in Real Time

home > Balsamiq Mockups 3 for Google Drive > Collaborating With Other Users in Real Time

Balsamiq Mockups 3 for Google Drive supports real-time collaboration between users. Just share the Mockups file with your collaborators, then have everyone launch the application on the same file. Everyone’s changes will be shared in real-time with all collaborators.

The other connected users will be shown in the top right of the screen. Hovering over their picture shows their email address.
